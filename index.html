Why "Potato"?

Because a potato doesn't judge you. A potato doesn't gasp when you brake a bit late. A potato doesn't give you a 45-minute lecture about the merits of heel-toe downshifting when you just wanted to learn how to parallel park.


We named this school after the humblest, most reliable, most universally beloved object on earth — because that's the energy we bring to every lesson. Solid. Dependable. No drama.


Also, we just really like the word potato.

"We put the TATER in navigator."
